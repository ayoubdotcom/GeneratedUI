Our Story

Founded in 2005 by Chef Marco Lombardi, Pizza Paradiso began as a small family-owned trattoria in the heart of Naples, Italy. With generations of pizza-making tradition in his blood, Chef Marco brought his authentic recipes and passion for quality ingredients to create something truly special.

What started as a humble neighborhood pizzeria has grown into a beloved local institution, known for our commitment to traditional techniques and innovative flavors. We still hand-toss every dough, slow-cook our tomato sauce for 48 hours, and source our mozzarella directly from small dairy farms in Campania.

At Pizza Paradiso, we believe that great pizza starts with great ingredients and ends with happy customers. Every pizza that leaves our kitchen carries with it our family's legacy and our dedication to the art of pizza-making.
